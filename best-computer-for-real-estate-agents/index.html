The realtor task list includes prospecting, paperwork, budgeting, finding the right clients, and then managing transactions. These will require a good computer to keep you on the go, and informed of new openings in different regions.

Similarly, there will be times when you will need to go live on Skype to close deals, and at times you have to keep your emails checked and respond to emails demanding your reply.

The display of this computer in view must have a good enough resolution to make your presentations easy and worth the client’s time. A computer that is not heavy, but portable enough to be carried just anywhere; is another expert tip for your computer need.

The ram size and storage will allow you to save up all your sample images and clients’ data, as well as back up your presentations and transaction. A good Ram will keep your computer running and free from lags, while a great battery will be the final icing on this cake.

Our top three picks of the best computer for real estate agents capture the highlighted needs above and provide a seasoned specialist opinion, handy for your smooth operation as a real estate agent.

Microsoft Surface Pro 6

1TB Computer For Real Estate Agents

Additional Images
Buy from Amazon.com
 

The 2-in-1 Microsoft Surface Pro 6 begins this list of fantastic computers for real estate agents, which is for many good reasons. The 12.3-inch convertible computer has a resolution of 2736×1824, offering a wide viewing angle and an adjustable kickstand.

The 8th generation Intel core i7 processor at a speed of 3.9GHz makes multitasking smooth on this computer. It allows you to research on the go and do other exciting things on your computer without dropping a sweat.

The 1TB storage space is the safest landing on this computer, with space to save just anything on the internet and a 16GB ram posed to give you the best computer experience, as a real estate agent.

There is a dedicated GPU intel UHD 620 graphics, ensuring your images turns out top-notch not blur, the efficiency of this device is bettered further down the line with its battery life lasting over 13.5 hours of usage.

On top of it, is the 1.77 pounds weight of this computer, which makes it a delight for every realtor needing a good computer.

The Acer TravelMate is specifically built for business-centred minds, like you the realtor seeking a device to expand your real estate business and provide better efficiency.

The obsidian black design of this computer makes it endearing and gives a perfect feel on the hands. The 8th generation Intel Core i5 processor powers this device, while the 8GB upgradeable ram offers just enough support for your multitasking and other real estate needs.

The 14-inch display, affords a much better viewing angle and display than the Surface Pro 6, with a dedicated graphics card to give you breathtaking clarity and bliss.

The battery last above 10 hours, and comes equipped with a fingerprint sensor. The storage is fixed at 256 GB SSD and has a backlight keypad to keep you connected at all times, even at night and in low light areas.

However, at 5.8 pounds, the Acer Travelmate can be a burden to carry around.

The 15.6-inch screen is a sterling feature of this Lenovo IdeaPad that gives it a commanding presence on this list, as one of the top three picks for best computers for real estate realtors.

The computer is powered by the 10th generation Intel Core i3 processor, keeping you updated with modern processor requirements. Also, with a processor speed, you can trust. The 1080p resolution and a 180-degree rotation are an amazing plus to this device and its avalanche of unique features.

An 8 GB of RAM and 256GB SSD, will provide enough space for all your storage needs and of course, give you the unrestricted freedom to navigate your tabs and process programs and apps without hitches or the fear of your computer slowing down.
