In such a situation, an under desk bike is the perfect answer.

It is very comfortable and helps you to exercise your legs while at work, at home, or at any convenient place really. It can also be used for health reasons to exercise your arms and legs and believe me when I say that there are many testimonies coming from those who have tried it. Exerpeutic 900E Under Desk Bike

Bluetooth Enabled Under Desk Bike

See larger image
Additional Images
Buy from Amazon.com
 

Using an under desk bike is very helpful to our mental and physical health but have you considered having an under desk bike with Bluetooth features? Well, the Exerpeutic under desk bike is about to blow your mind.

This under desk bike uses Bluetooth and the MyCloudFitness app with an online database that helps you keep track of your progress. The app is available on iOS and Android and it tracks time, distance, speed, calories burned, and your number of turns per minute. With the MyCloudFitness app, you have unlimited access to your past data that contains your progress so far and you can equally set three workout goal settings of time, distance, and burned calories to help you do better.

Another great feature of this under desk bike is its adjustable wheel holder that can be extended to accommodate tall people and prevent their knees from hitting the desk while pedaling. The wheel holder also has an extendable chair hook that is easy to roll-on and lock and thus eliminating the possibility of pedaling too far from the bike.

Another amazing feature employed by this under desk bike is its 14 level of magnetic tension resistance and its 10 inch pedal height that can be adjusted to comfortably accommodate its user. During use, this under desk bike is very quiet and it has smooth wheels and a firm handle for easy carriage.

Even with a tight schedule at work, you can still keep up with your fitness goal using the MaxKare under desk bike. It is easy to use and can be used for exercising the arms, and legs. This under desk bike comes with a detachable resistance rope that can also be used to exercise.

It has 16 levels of magnetic resistance that can be easily adjusted to suit the needs of the user. The pedals of this under desk bike employ quality anti-slip technology and can be adjusted to provide low impact exercise to the user without compromising comfort and stability while it is being used.

This under desk bike also features a digital LCD Monitor that displays time, distance, speed, and calories burned. This feature is employed to help you keep track of your progress and motivate you to do better. MaxKare under desk bike is very portable via a firm carrying handle and it also has a protective pad on its bottom that makes it very sturdy to ensure a smooth and safe exercise.

Deceli Under Desk Bike

Budget-Friendly Under Desk Bike

Yes, I was surprised as you when I saw the price of this under desk bike and the wonderful thing is that it is highly functional and very durable. Deceli under desk bike is made from quality steel and plastic and functions with a AAA battery. Its resistance can be easily adjusted through its knob to suit the needs of the user and this makes it highly suitable for arms and legs exercise.

The weight capacity of the peddler is 220lbs and it has a 5 pounds flywheel that is made of steel. Deceli under desk bike features four anti-slip rubber pedals at its base with adjustable foot straps that can comfortably support different feet sizes. It also has an extra strap to provide more security to its users.

As reputed to most functional and top quality under desk bikes, Deceli under desk bike also features an LCD monitor that helps you to keep track of your progress by displaying the distance, time, number of turns, and the number of calories that have been burnt.

It can be easily used under desks whose height is above 47 inches. Despite its pocket-friendly price, this under desk bike is highly functional for joints and muscle exercise, stimulating blood circulation and helping you perform gentle low impact exercise while at work.
