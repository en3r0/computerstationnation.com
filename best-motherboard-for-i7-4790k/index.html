A motherboard is also known as the mainboard, system board, planar board, or logic board. The motherboard is the backbone and the primary piece of the circuitry of a computer. It ties the components together at one spot allows them to interact with one another.

To determine the best motherboard for i7 4790k depends on whether the system will be used to play games or not. For gamers who are very competitive, the best motherboard is the MSI Z97 Gaming 5 and MSI Z87-G45 for non-competitive gamers and the Asus ROG Maximus VII Formula.

MSI Z97 Gaming 5

2PCIe x 16 slots Motherboard For i7 4790K

Additional Images

The MSI Z97 Gaming 5 uses DDR3 memory type with maximum speeds of up to 2400 MHz and 4 DDR3 slots allowing for a maximum total of 32 GB RAM. It has 6 SATA 3.0 hard drive slots which allow for theoretical transfer speeds up to 6GB as opposed to the 3GB of SATA 2.0. It also supports onboard graphics which allows for integrated graphics.

There are 2PCIe x 16 slots on this motherboard and can accommodate the latest graphics card interface of PCIe v3.0 as anything below will not reach the motherboard’s potential and anything above will have its performance slashed to the bandwidth maximum of the MSI Z97 GAMING 5’s PCIe v3.0.

It also has 6 USB 2.0 slots and 5 USB 3.0 slots. USB 3.0 slots are theoretically capable of data transfer speeds of up to 5 Gbit/s, which is over 10 times faster than the USB 2.0 top speed of 480 Mbit/s. It keeps a nice efficiency regarding cost, quality, and performance and allows a person to have more control over the overclocking of the CPU and a much stronger overclocking of the RAM up to 3300MHz, a better audio boost, it supports M.2 and up to the 5th generation of processors.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Good audio performance
  • A stronger overclocking
  • Promotes gaming traffic.

Cons

  • There is no integrated Wifi
  • There is no integrated Bluetooth.

MSI Z87-G45

2 Nvidia SLI Motherboard For i7 4790K

See larger image
Additional Images

The MSI Z87–G45 has 4 RAM entries, supports socket 1150, USB 3.0, and allows overclocking. It can easily overclock. Conforming to the ATX standard, the MSI Z87-G45 fits in most cases because the ATX is the most common form factor, and as such has a high degree of compatibility with other components while providing a decent number of slots to widen the expansion options.

This motherboard has 6 SATA 3.0 hard drive slots which allow for theoretical data transfer speeds up to 6GB/s. however, only high-performance hard drives, specifically solid-state drives, will be able to take advantage of the bandwidth potential of SATA 3.0 ports.

It is advisable to use a graphics card with the same graphics card interface as PCIe v3.0 because anything below will not maximize this motherboard’s potential. Anything above will have the motherboard performance slashed to the bandwidth maximum of the MSI Z87-G45 PCIe v3.0.

It also supports up to 2 Nvidia SLI-connected graphics cards or up to 3 AMD Crossfire connected graphics cards for improved overall graphical performance at the cost of multiplied graphics-based power consumption and the cards’ price themselves.

It is great for people who want to game without all the twerking because it has one-click automatic overclocking software. It has a gaming device that optimized with triple gold – plating for high polling rate gaming devices.

Conclusion

It is referred to as the mother of all components attached to it. Without the motherboard, the computer cannot function. The CPU, GPU, optical drives, video card, or hard drive could interact. The motherboard looks like a flat plastic with a lot of wires attached to it.

Its parts include power and data connectors, capacitors, heat sinks, and fans. The motherboard acts as the platform on which various expansion slots are available to install other devices. It also distributes power to various computer components and is used in the coordination of the various devices in the computer and maintains an interface among them.

Intel Core i7 4790k is a model CPU socket type tech spec. It is manufactured by Intel and was first made available on the 2nd June 2014. For a motherboard to be suited for i7 4790k, it must have the LGA 1150 CPU socket that the Intel Core i7 4790k 4- Core 4.0GHz will fit into, providing motherboard to CPU compatibility.

Considering the Gigabyte Z97X Gaming 5 with its M.2 connector, a SATA Express 10Gb/s port, PCIe x16, and USB 3.0 is the best gaming motherboard in the market compared to the MSI Z87-G45 and Asus Maximus VII Formula.

A motherboard is one of the most difficult parts of a computer to repair. Most motherboards outlast the rest of the computer parts if they are well maintained. It is advisable to use approved accessories and power devices so as they avoid the above-mentioned motherboard killers.
