Using an under desk bike is very helpful to our mental and physical health but have you considered having an under desk bike with Bluetooth features? Well, the Exerpeutic under desk bike is about to blow your mind.

This under desk bike uses Bluetooth and the MyCloudFitness app with an online database that helps you keep track of your progress.
