]]> Hp is one of the most trusted brands for office and computer equipment, and over the years, they’ve proven to consumers that their products are durable and built with features that ensure excellent performance. One of the top computer office equipment produced by Hp is computer printers, and for this context, we’ll focus on computer printers suitable for use in a home office. 

Hp printers have notable features that make them suitable for handling tasks such as printing, scanning, copying, and even mobile fax. The user-friendly design of Hp printers is aided by the Hp smart app that makes set up, printing, copying, scanning, installing drivers, loading papers, and installing ink cartridges very easy. 

Hp printers come with the alternative of signing up with the Hp instant ink/toner service to ensure that you never run out of ink, and they also come with a minimum of a one-year warranty. 

Hp printers function at a very low noise to eliminate distraction when working and thus make them perfect for offices both at home and in a professional setting.  If you have plans of investing in a Hp printer for your home office, the best place to begin your search is with our top 3 picks for the best Hp computer printer for your home office.

The Hp Pro 2655 is designed as a wireless all-in-one printer suitable for professional and home offices. It has printing, scanning, and copying capabilities that function via a wireless network and through the Hp smart app that allows for easy utilization of the printer’s features from a smartphone’s convenience. 

This printer prints high-quality documents in both monochrome and colored format. It has an impressive printing speed of 7.5ppm when printing documents in a black and white format and 5.5ppm when printing documents in a colored format. 

It has a 25-sheets document feeder that can be closed to save space and a paper tray that supports various paper sizes that include the letter, legal, 4X6 inches, 5X7 inches, 8X10 inches, and envelopes. 

Its high-end features prevent jam, and it supports easy printing from mobile devices via the Hp smart app that allows you to set up the printer easily, scan from a smartphone, order, toner, and copying from Cloud, Dropbox, and Google Drive. The Hp Pro 2655 is highly functional, durable, supports fast printing, and comes with a one-year limited hardware warranty.
