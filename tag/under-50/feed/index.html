A CPU cooler is a device designed to draw heat away from the CPU and other components of the computer. The computer uses a CPU cooler to lower CPU temperatures improves efficiency and stability of the system and can increase the overall noise level of the system.

There are two types of CPU coolers, they are air CPU coolers and liquid CPU coolers. Air CPU cooler may consist of a heat sink or a combination of a heat sink and fan.

A heat sink is a component of the computer that is designed to lower the temperature of an electronic device by dissipating heat into the surrounding air.
