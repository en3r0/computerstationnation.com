The Canon Pixma TS8220 wireless printer is an all-in-one photo printer that supports scanning, and copying alongside the printing of high-end photos in outstanding picture quality. It has a six-color individual ink system that supports instant printing of stunning photos on glossy papers in premium picture quality with a printing speed of seventeen seconds.
