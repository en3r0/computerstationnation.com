Corsair Obsidian 750D

Tempered Glass Computer Case For Nas

Built as a full tower computer case, the Corsair Obsidian 750D  offers outstanding power, flexibility, and storage capacity that can fully accommodate a large number of NAS drives, as well as easy and fast upgrading of hardware. 

It is equipped with nine PCI-E expansion slots that support double and triple GPUs on large motherboards,  four separate mounting locations that support removable 3.5” or 2.5” drive cages, ten drives, and  Four tool-free dedicated SSD cages. 

The Corsair Obsidian 750D uses an air cooling feature that supports six case fans including three 140mm pre-installed intake and exhaust RGB LED  fans that are custom engineered to deliver excellent airflow with beautiful lighting effects that can be controlled with an LED controller. In addition to the air cooling feature, it also supports a water cooling system that supports dual radiators on the bottom, front, and top of the case. 

Furthermore, this case supports a 450mm GPU, a 170mm CPU cooler, a removable dust filter, and an ATX, E-ATX, micro ATX, and ITX motherboard form factor.

The Corsair Obsidian 750D is built with a tempered glass side panel, and it features excellent cable management with built-in cable routing channels, rubber grommets, and included Velcro cable straps that provide neat visibility of the PC’s components. It employs a tool-free storage installation, four USB ports on the front panels, and comes with a two-year warranty.
