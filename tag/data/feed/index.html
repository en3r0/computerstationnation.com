]]> A data analyst deals with a lot of data. Being a data analyst, you have to collect, analyze and interpret data in ways that will be useful to businesses. To handle the many data that the data analyst comes in contact with, he needs a computer that works very efficiently. The efficiency of the laptop or computer will make the work go smoothly.

Software for data analysis such as IBM, SPSS, Statistics software requires a minimum of 4GB RAM to work effectively. Working with laptops that have the minimum RAM will, however, cause slowness in your operations; in other words, it is not advised to work with the minimum RAM.

In essence, you will need a computer with a high-end specification to effectively process your data and develop results that will be useful to your client timeously. 

A computer, whether a desktop computer or laptop for data analysis, should have these qualities. Large RAM size, large storage, quality processor, and GPU for some data. The laptop should have a superb keyboard and a screen size of at least 13 inches.
