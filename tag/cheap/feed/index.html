Cheap Desk Questions

How much does a desk cost?

Most computer desks cost anywhere from $100 to $1,000. The price depends on the materials used, the size, and the layout.

Where can I buy an inexpensive desk?

You can find them in big box stores like Wal-Mart, IKEA, and Target. You can also find them online at Amazon.

What is the cheapest gaming desk?

We recommend the Atlantic Gaming – Original as the best cheap gaming desk.

How long will a cheap desk last?

If you treat it right, a cheap desk can last a very long time. I had a desk from Wal-Mart that lasted me over 10 years.
