If you are looking for a more unconventional router that looks trendy and doesn’t even burn a hole in your pocket, the Google Nest Wi-Fi system is probably the best appliance to invest in. For starters, this is one of the most reliable units to cut out the dead zones from expansive home and office spaces. Besides that, this device allows you to pair additional devices as Wi-Fi extenders for increasing the coverage from 2200sqft to almost 3800sqft.

Precisely a mesh system, this AC2200 gadget is all about speeds and throughput exceeding 2000mbps. In addition to that, this system also comes with support for 2 gig LAN ports for exceptional wired connectivity and performances. The Nest router system is compatible with the Google App and Google Wi-Fi and makes sure that the device firmware is periodically upgraded without manual interventions. 

Although this is one of the more aesthetic routers on our list, the primary reason for featuring the same is the advanced security system. Besides protecting the user data, the Nest Wi-Fi also comes with efficient parental controls for restricting certain online activities.
