This compact mechanical keyboard has a tenkeyless design that supports a vibrant gaming atmosphere. It’s large and well textured FPS and MOBA keys feature full key rollover on USB and 100% anti-ghosting features that provide accuracy when gaming. This keyboard also has a USB pass-through that is well-positioned to give you an uninterrupted gaming session. It uses 100% Cherry MX Red Switches with gold contacts for fast, and precise keypresses.

Another interesting feature of this keyboard is it’s vibrant five level multicolor RGB lightings. The LED backlights are very impressive and can be adjusted to three intensity levels and in a situation where you wish to turn it off, you can do so. The Corsair K65 Lux RGB proudly boasts of iCue that allows for customizable advanced macro and lighting programming.

To ensure full control of your gaming arena, the windows key can be deactivated, providing you with uninterrupted thrilling sessions. All the jaw-dropping features are enclosed in an aircraft-grade anodized brushed aluminum frame that attests to its durability.

This mechanical keyboard is attached to a lapboard, plus it is wireless and this makes it very easy to carry around and use comfortably. The keyboard has a tool-free design that makes the keyboard easily detachable from the lapboard for single use without hindering the functionality of the mouse pad.

Take a bigger and clearer step towards winning with the Corsair k55 gaming keyboard and mouse combo. This keyboard boasts of a 6000 DPI performance gaming mouse sensor and you do not need to modify the mouse sensitivity through the control panel. The mouse also has beautiful customizable RGB backlighting options. It has molded rubber grips by the side that allows you to confidently hold the mouse.

The keyboard and mouse combo is designed with gaming features to help you take the lead when gaming. It has six programmable macro keys that enables key remaps and key combos for executing complex actions. It also has a detachable wrist rest that is designed with soft rubber texture that provides a comfortable position for long and intense gaming hours.

An added feature to this keyboard is the multimedia and volume keys that allows you to easily control and adjust sounds without interrupting your game. The RGB backlighting is also worthy of applause. It is three zoned with over ten preconfigured vivid RGB lighting modes that you can freely choose from.
