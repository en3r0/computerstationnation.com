If you are looking for a Wi-Fi extender that relies more on the outdoor range without compromising on speed and connection quality, the TP-Link CPE210 is the product to opt for. Conclusion

The best Wi-Fi extender for outside needs to be rugged and sleek, to be able to withstand harshest possible weather conditions! While each one of the three gadgets fit the description, it is necessary to ascertain the exact set of requirements before taking the final call.

If you are looking for power and coverage area, the TP-Link CPE210 is the product to consider. If you are looking for a dual-band, basic plug-and-play model, featuring omnidirectional antennas, the WAVLINK AC1200 should be prioritized. 

Lastly, if you have a smaller outdoor space to cover and speed of transmission is your preference, the TP-Link EAP225 is a pretty handy gadget, best characterized by a remote management software and a dust proof and waterproof design.
