The BenQ Screenbar E-Reading LED Monitor comes with aluminum material, weighing 1.17 pounds. It is designed to stable mount on the monitor. With a thickness from 0.4 to 1.2 (1 to 3 cm), the gadget fits nearly any monitor.

Monitors with a bezel wider than 0.8cm (0.32) are the best fit for the device thus; valuable space is created giving your desk space a stylish look.

Using a built–in ambient light sensor, the screen bar makes for an intelligent brightness thus, relieving eye fatigue. Though the gadget features an auto–dimming experience, the screen bar also makes room to dim the light through its touch control sensor manually.

The BenQ Screenbar comes with a hue adjustment feature. The adjustment highlights the color temperature to provide focus and comfort. Its warm light puts you in a relaxed mood while the cool light provides better concentration, giving you work efficiency.

Conclusion

Though they perform a similar function with almost the same feature, the devices mentioned above are different in quality and meet specific needs.

If you want a monitor light bar you can move around, and great for a student, the Cyezcor LED Desk Lamp suits that purpose. Fairly speaking, it is not that costly.

However, if you are the type that spends hours on the monitor screen, especially if you are an onscreen reader who reads for long, then the Quintus E-Reading Task Lamp saves the day for you.

It is the go-to device because it is the best in terms of relieving eye fatigue. This device is a bargain for the price.

If you are a programmer, a gamer, or even an on-screen reader, the BenQScreenBar E-Reading LED monitor fits these purposes. Its lasting life span and 14 level brightness make for good illumination and an intelligent brightness.

Similarly, the primary purpose of monitoring light bars is to improve your working environment’s lighting condition. However, the monitor light bars come in variant modes- the screen light bars, which are mounted, on the screen, and the ones, which are affixed to a surface.

The best pick would be the screen light bars as it offers a better and wide range illumination.

Furthermore, in purchasing a monitor light bar, whether for your desktop space or laptop, features such as adjustable angles to avoid light reflection and screen glare, adjustable color temperature to suit your mood, and a design to match your need should be considered.

The only negative here is the pricing, but if your budget can suit the offer, it would be a good buy. This device is our preferred choice.
