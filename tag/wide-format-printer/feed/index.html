The computer printer is an output device, it generates text and graphics on a physical medium like on paper. The generated texts are called prints, also referred to as hard copy. Unlike the Visual Display Unit (VDU) that brings out its output on the monitor, the printer’s output is on paper.

Wide-format printing is relatively new compared to the other types of printing. This printing method can be used for the following projects, posters, banners, wallpapers, and murals, laminating, and trade show graphics – the list is not exhaustive. Conclusion

Since the discovery of printers, they have evolved from the first printing being covered on wood to stones to paper. Wide Format or large format printers are one of the results of evolution.

If you do printing of banners, posters, trade show graphics, wallpapers, mural, and the likes or you are about to go into it, it is essential you have a wide format all in one printer. The regular printer will not be able to do the printing job for you that a wide-format printer can do for you.

When it comes to printing, there are different types of printing and it’s still evolving. Here are some of the most popular ones; offset lithography, flexography, digital printing, wide-format printing, screen printing, 3D Printing, and LED UV.

Wide-format printing, also called large format printing refers to printing that requires specialized printing equipment that can accommodate larger printing projects.

You have set out to get a wide format printer, here are what you should look out for. Consider a printer with multiple paper trays, scanning abilities, and a printer with plenty of connectivity options.
