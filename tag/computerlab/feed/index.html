Conclusion 

Using headphones in an elementary school computer lab is a great way of sustaining the focus of students. At the same time, they learn by eliminating external noises that could distract them from learning.

Apart from the excitement shared by the kids in a computer lab, it is also distracting to listen to the different or uniform sounds from each computer while trying to learn or execute a task assigned by the teacher.

A tested and trusted way to prevent such occurrence and simultaneously sustain the kids’ focus in the computer lab is to use a headphone. Headphones are a necessity in elementary school computer labs because they create a more audible lesson and a quieter learning environment where the focus on individual tasks can be maintained.
