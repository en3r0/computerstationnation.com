If you are seeking the best wireless router for streaming 4K, it is appropriate to go with the Linksys Max-Stream EA9500, boasting of the AC5400 standards. Besides offering extensive coverage of up to 3000sqft, this appliance also allows you to connect at least 25 devices, at once. Moreover, the Tri-Band functionality is also a handy resource for the 4K streamers, allowing them to deploy the additional bandwidth and speeds that reach up to 5400mbps.

From a structural point of view, this appliance resembles a drone with 8 antennas adding to the routing capabilities of the same. When it comes to the internals, there is a dual-core processor at the helm. What stands out though is the built-in Max-Stream extender that ensures unmatched throughput and minimal signal drops.

However, it is the availability of 8 Gigabit ports that steals the show courtesy of the 10x faster, wired connections via every single slot. In addition to that, this powerful router also supports the MU-MIMO technology that is the primary concept behind simultaneous connections.

For budget-centric 4K streamers, the Nighthawk R7000 from Netgear is the best possible selection. While there aren’t many performance tradeoffs when it comes to making it more affordable as compared to some of the other products on our list, it comes with a slightly underpowered dual-core processor that isn’t as equipped as the one in the Nighthawk R9000.

However, the standout attributes include the extensive wireless coverage of up to 1800sqft followed by a cumulative speed that can go up to 1900mbps. Despite being a dual-band router, the processing throughput is at par with the tri-band contemporaries. This router is capable of connecting 30 devices at once while ensuring the best band allotment for the concerned gadgets, depending on the distance.

When it comes to the best set of features, this affordable router from Netgear comes equipped with four gig Ethernet ports for faster, wired connections. Apart from that, Netgear ensures a seamless app-based setup followed by the inclusion of parental control and the class-leading Armor support for keeping network-specific threats at bay.

Conclusion

Each one of the three routers is equipped with class-leading wireless technologies that make room for faster and lag-free 4K streaming. However, the final selection depends on your budget and personal or professional preferences. For instance, if you are seeking the highest possible speeds, responsive UI, VR readiness, and a brand-centric validation, the Netgear Nighthawk R9000 is the one to consider.

Then again, if your secondary requirement is diversified wired connectivity, the Linksys Max-Stream is the best wireless router for streaming 4K. Lastly, for the budget users who aren’t exactly upbeat about tri-band technologies, the Nighthawk R7000 from Netgear is the perfect buying option.
