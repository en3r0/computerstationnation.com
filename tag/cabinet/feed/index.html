Review

This L-shaped desk from Monarch Specialties is a perfect workstation courtesy of the sturdy and premium layout. Firstly, this is an extremely spacious desk with an equivalent length of almost 80 inches. Secondly, the metallic legs included within the package are sleek and do not interfere with the visible craftsmanship. The only area of improvement that’s visible is the lack of holes in the existing product. The company would have been better off with an optimized structure with holes, specifically for concealing and keeping the wires untangled, in case electronic devices, monitors, and printers are to be accommodated.

Be it the overall finishing or the unmatched affordability, this product excels in several departments. The available work surface is durable and thick paneled whereas the filing cabinet is compact yet adequate enough for a general user.
