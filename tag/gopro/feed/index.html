Conclusion

While the site lists items such as; 4GB ram, 1280×800, and 64bit as the minimum system requirements of ram, display resolution, and operating system, respectively.

To get the best out of the GoPro platform you will require a system that doubles the minimum system requirement, with advanced options.

Video editing is a graphics-intensive computer operation, and of course will demand much from your GPU, while this is not expressly stated on the minimum system requirement.

If you are not a beginner, you already know this is a feature you cannot overlook. For the beginner, we got you covered, so have no fears.

Here are a few things to look out for, if you are to enjoy the complete options of a computer that meets the terms of GoPro and provides the best results;

  1. Processor: to run GoPro smoothly, you will require a processor with a good clock speed, and some cores, this is essential and will determine how fast or slow your computer runs.
  2. GPU: Video editing on its own requires the right graphics to render at its best and this is an essential component of every computer; a computer with a good rendering time is pure bliss.
  3. Memory: this covers the ram and storage, there are essential and will determine how great your computer operates, with a good load speed and how fast you can process information.

Similarly, GoPro videos are often storage-intensive, with some 1-hour videos occupying as much as 50GB of space, so the right storage is necessary.

4. Display: this should be at the top of this list, your Display resolution will determine how your GoPro program appears on your computer and if you do not have the right display; it will affect your productivity. Therefore, this is an essential feature to look at.

For your smooth and easy GoPro operations, the ASUS ROG GL700 is best recommended for unhindered access and productivity, this computer offers enough specifications for just anything you would want to throw at it.For other computers in this category, you can check Amazon.
