HD Audio Computer Amplifier

As a gamer, one of your needs includes an enhanced audio system to help you enjoy your games and this computer amplifier embodies all such features that are geared towards delivering exceptional bass performance, acoustic clarity, and precise positional audios for an immersive gaming experience.

The Epos audio amplifier is built with high-end audio technological features that enable it to deliver excellent audios that are highly suitable for gaming and enterprise.  It functions with an HD audio engine, separate audio and communication control, built-in DAC, and  7.1 surround sound mode.

Its features can be easily customized to focus on the sound frequencies in specific areas with convenient presets where they’re needed. It also employs features that allow for easy switching between audio modes, sound balance, separate control of game audios and chats, and shuffling between headset and speaker profiles.  One of such features is the sidetone feature that allows users to control and fine-tune the loudness of their voice in the headset.

Furthermore, this computer amplifier employs an LED panel that is sensor activated to control quick and easy switching between audio modes without exiting the game.

It also features a settings kick that is designed to prevent accidental audio changes. The integrated LED feature functions with a large volume ring that allows for quick fingertip control on sound levels. The Epos computer amplifier is compatible with PC, Mac, desktop, laptop, and all two-fingertip headsets/ headphones.

Fiio Q1 Mark II

Xmos Platform Computer Amplifier

This computer amplifier is equipped with the XMOS platform that functions to support easy decoding of sound formats with up to 384kHz /32 bit PCM and DSD256 with outstanding sound performance. It doubles as a headphone amplifier with an approved OPA926 audiophile and a DAC that decodes high sampling rate formats.

Being equipped with the XMOS platform allows the Q1 Mark II to support decoding of more than one format with outstanding performance through a DAC that makes it capable of decoding up to 384 kHz /32 bit PCM as well as up to DSD256.

The Fiio Q1 Mark II is MFI certified and this makes it easily compatible with all versions of iOS. It is also certified as a Hi-Res Audio that is capable of a naturalistic and realistic listening experience.

It features a volume knob that is built with an obconical design that makes it easy to rotate while still fitting it to limited space. Together with this switch are others that are built for adjusting gain, and fine-tuning bass levels.

This computer amplifier has a single-ended and balanced headphone output that produces two different types of audio sounds that are pleasing to the senses. It employs a USB interface that automatically determines the type of device that it is paired with. 

It is also equipped with a lighting-to-micro USB short cable, micro USB cable, long data cable, 3.5mm audio cable, 2x long silicone bands, 2x short silicone bands, silicone pad, and a carrying pouch for easy portability.
