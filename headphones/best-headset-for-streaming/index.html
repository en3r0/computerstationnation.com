Best Headset For Streaming

When you buy through links on our site, we may earn an affiliate commission. Learn more.

Irrespective of the type of streaming or the app that you intend to use for it, your audio quality is one major factor that can affect your quality, and one of the ways that it does is through your choice of headset. Using a headset that can efficiently cater to your streaming tactics is of great importance because it can affect your output in a positive or negative way (what am I saying is nobody wants a bad audio output).

Another important feature of a headset that can assist your audio quality when streaming is a headset that has a good surround sound (preferably 7.1 surround sound). Good surround sound enclosed in a sturdy headset made of durable materials that can comfortably support you for extended hours is ideal for streaming because it can make a lot of positive differences for smooth communication and superior audio quality.

If you’re hoping to buy a highly functional headset for steaming all the things, then you should check out our top 3 picks for the best headset for streaming. They are all great for this purpose obviously and have nice features to cater to your needs. Let’s dive in!

Razer Kraken Kitty RGB Gaming Headset

Wired Headset For Streaming

See larger image
Additional Images
Buy from Amazon.com
 

Razer Kraken headset employs a 7.1 surround sound for accurate positional and directional sounds that are highly appreciated in streaming styles, plus the sounds are clear and loud.

The design of this headset is a beauty to behold and the colorful ambient of this headset ears is credited to its stream responsive RGB lighting via the Razer Chroma technology that employs 16.8 million kitty ear lighting color options that the user can choose from to set up his preferred style combination.

Razer Kraken Kitty headset is very comfortable for an extended period of use because it employs cooling-gel ear cushioning and a bauxite aluminum-framed headband that can be easily adjusted to fit any head size. The bauxite aluminum frame contributes to the lightweight, sturdiness, and durability of the headset. It also employs THX spatial audio and this makes it an ideal choice for extended usage for activities such as streaming, gaming, and action-packed movies. Its ear cups have buttons for volume adjustment and THX Spatial on and off toggle.

It also employs a retractable microphone that uses noise-canceling technology to eliminate distractions in the form of background noise and provide clear audio when streaming. The microphone is well built to instantly pick up your voice so that it comes out loud and clear.

This headset is available in different colors and it has a frequency range of 20Hz -20kHz.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • The ear cups have customizable colors
  • It has a noise-canceling retractable microphone
  • It has 16.8 million RGB lighting colors

Cons

  • The THX Spatial Audio technology is only available on Windows 10 64 bit
  • It is expensive

Corsair HS7 Pro

Wireless Headset for Steaming

See larger image
Additional Images
Buy from Amazon.com
 

Corsair HS7 Pro is a wireless headset that employs 2.4GHz wireless technology to connect with enabled devices within 40 feet and it has a frequency of 29Hz to 20kHz. This headset is lightweight and has adjustable ear cups that are well padded with plush memory foam to deliver exceptional comfort during extended hours of streaming.

Its thrilling and superior sound quality is credited to its 50mm neodymium audio driver that is well enhanced to deliver sounds with well-filtered bass. This headset also features a unidirectional noise-canceling detachable microphone with a frequency of 100Hz to 10kHzt that skillful eliminates background noise to ensure clarity in your voice. The microphone also features a flip-up mute function and a built-in LED mute indicator that notifies its user of the audio state of the microphone.

Corsair HS7 Pro utilizes 7.1 surround sound that is highly suitable for streaming because it provides directional sounds for great sound imaging and it also creates a multichannel audio experience. It also utilizes Corsair iCUE software for adjusting custom audio equalizer settings, side tone control, and virtual surround audio. This wireless headset is durable and has a battery that can power it for up to 16 hours. It is compatible with PCs and PS4.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • It features 50mm neodymium audio drivers
  • It has a unidirectional microphone
  • It employs 7.1 surround sound

Cons

  • Its 7.1 surround sound can only be activated on PC
  • It does not have a long-lasting battery

Docooler HUHD Wireless Headset

Budget-Friendly Headset for Steaming

Additional Images

This headset is ergonomically built with a stretchable headband  that can be easily adjusted to a perfect fit according to the preference of its user. It also features large soft ear cups that are well padded with plush memory foam to provide all-round comfort and relief during extended working hours. 

Docooler HUHD headset employs optical wireless connectivity via an optical fiber wire that utilizes a 2.4Ghz optical wireless connection to pair with devices within 32 feet to deliver high-quality sounds. This headset employs 7.1 surround sound for high-end sound imaging and directional sounds. It also features a detachable noise-canceling microphone that silences background noise and ensures a smooth and clear audio experience. The microphone is omnidirectional and has a frequency range of 100Hz to 8KHz.

This headset also boasts of a long-lasting battery that charges via a micro USB cable and provides power that can last up to 15 hours on a full charge.

It is compatible with PS3, PS4, PCs, Mac, tablets, and laptops.

  • It has an omnidirectional noise-canceling microphone
  • Its earcups are well padded with memory foam
