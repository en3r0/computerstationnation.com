Apart from the luxury of not being chained to your desk by cables, a wireless mouse is a neater and portable option that chases away your fear of tangled mouse cables. It is a simple gaming device that is highly effective and does the job accurately. These days, the latency times are basically non-existent! However, if you prefer the wired variety, check out the best overall gaming mice here.

Lightweight Gaming Mouse

Additional Images
Buy from Amazon.com
 

Create a lag-free gaming session with the Razer Basilisk X hyper-speed gaming mouse that allows for dual-mode connectivity. It does so via Bluetooth for efficient power consumption and Razer hyper-speed wireless transmission mode that features a low latency, seamless frequency switching, and interference reduction.

This mouse has six programmable buttons that can be assigned complex macro functions through the Razer Synapse 3 software. The battery life is top-notch and can serve you wholeheartedly for up to 450 hours when on Bluetooth and 285 hours when it is on hyper speed wireless mode.

Also worth mentioning is its mechanical switches that have a lifespan of 50 million clicks and the two years warranty that covers for damage and malfunctioning during use.

Redragon M913

Optical MMO Mouse

See larger image
Additional Images
Buy from Amazon.com
 

If you’re hoping to get a mouse that allows you to easily switch between wireless and wired mode, then this is a very nice option in the mouse market. The dual-mode supports a 40G acceleration that can be easily activated using a 2.4 GHz wireless connectivity, and a wired mode that supports a USB Type C wired mode.

The optical sensor features by this gaming mouse are fast and register each click at the speed of light. The advanced sensor consumes very little power and can be used for up to 80 hours when in ECO mode that helps to reduce the usage of it’s 16.8 million customizable backlit lighting colors. This feature makes the mouse go into sleep mode after detecting inactivity for nine minutes.Another worthy feature of this mouse is its five-speed adjustable DPI that can be easily switched in-between to a model that suits the current need of the user with an exclusive DPI setting that can be personalized. 

The MMO side buttons labeled 1-12 are responsive and are resistant to fade. This mouse is highly efficient for gaming and it’s low latency and keyboard shortcuts attest to this fact.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • It has battery saving features
  • It has a personalized DPI setting that can be switched to and fro
  • It can be used in wired or wireless mode

Cons

  • The DPI buttons cannon be remapped
  • The side buttons require more pressure to press

Pictek Wireless Gaming Mouse

Ergonomic Hybrid Mouse

See larger image
Additional Images
Buy from Amazon.com
 

The performance of this wireless mouse is worthy of applause. Its wireless connection is enabled via a USB receiver and it can also alternate as a wired mouse via its 5.2 feet USB Type C cable which also doubles as its rechargeable plug. Its inbuilt 900 mAh battery can last up to 36 hours with the RGB lights and 48 hours if the lights are off. The RGB lighting colors are accessible in three zones that can be alternatively switched or brightened.

It has a six-speed adjustable DPI that functions with color indicators for each level. The mouse also features a color indicator for low battery, full battery, out of power, and charging mode. It has eight programmable buttons that are easily customizable and can be assigned functions for quick access to specific actions, hence a faster and effective inputting. Another gaming enhanced feature of this mouse is its fire button that is highly effective for FPS battles as well as MMO, and MOBA games.

The ergonomic design of this rechargeable wireless mouse features a thumb rest and a rubberized scroll wheel that mimics the natural right-hand position when gaming. It is largely compatible with Windows and Mac Operating Systems. However, the side buttons and the programming software are not available in Mac Operating Systems. This rechargeable wireless mouse is functional with a sweat-resistant matte finish for a secure and firmer grip.

Pros and Cons

Pros 

  • It has an ergonomic design and is resistant to sweat
  • It can be used in wired and wireless mode
  • It has colorful RGB lighting

Cons

  • The RGB lights go off when it is in wireless mode
  • The battery doesn’t last very long especially when the RGB lights are on
