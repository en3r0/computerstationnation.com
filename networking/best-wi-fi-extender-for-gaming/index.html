Online gaming indulgences are necessary to kill time, especially when you are home-bound and looking to break free from the monotony of professional tasks.

However, before you invest time and effort into planning a home-centric online setup for a Massively Multiplayer Online game like World Of Warcraft, Battle Royale games like PUBG or Fortnite, or even a Multiplayer Online Battle Arena like League of Legends, it’s advisable to check for the wifi dead zones in the vicinity.

A Wi-Fi dead zone immediately disconnects you from the online world and can negatively impact your game-playing experiences. This is why you must consider the best Wi-Fi extender for Gaming that can complement high-end routers and gaming cards. In the subsequent sections, we shall enlist the best Wi-Fi extenders for gaming, with a focus on the efficacy of the Ethernet port, existing wireless standard, and the number for antennas.

If you are seeking a functional Wi-Fi extender that scales above the rest in terms of range, coverage area, and a number of simultaneous connections, this masterpiece of Netgear is worth considering. For starters, this is the best Wi-Fi extender for gaming, provided you have are looking to cover almost 2300Sqft. Not just that, you can easily connect up to 40 devices at once, without compromising on the strength and speed.

When it comes to speed, this gaming extender is capable of reaching upon 2200Mbps. The dual-band compatibility makes it the best router in the given price segment. Not just that, the gadget deliberately refrains from using external antennas as the internal array is extremely capable when range enhancement is concerned. Not just that, the Gig Ethernet port accounts for the fastest connections and the one-touch gadget setup tab deserves a special mention.

However, the mesh networking support is what steals the show, courtesy of the FastLane technology. This innovation amalgamates the existing bands and makes way for unmatched streaming speeds and roaming connectivity. Most importantly, the AC2200 wireless standard is backward compatible with almost every gateway, router, and Wi-Fi card.

Linksys RE7000

See larger image
Additional Images
Buy from Amazon.com
 

Best suited towards diverse professional online gaming requirements, this innovative product from Linksys isn’t just a good extender but also works as a potent repeater or rather Wi-Fi booster. When it comes to connectivity, this appliance can readily eliminate all the dead zones inside the home and office. The dual Wi-Fi band works like charm and makes use of the MU-MIMO wireless technology. 

This gadget allows you to play online games without any interruption, even if you aren’t near the wireless router. Based on the AC1900 wireless standard, this extender works best when paired with the AC1900 router. However, the performances are equally good for other wireless standards. This extender also features the innovative Spot Finder innovation which makes it easier for you to identify dead zones and place the gadget, according to the same.

The main reason for putting this on our list is the massive 10,000sqft coverage and the plug-and-play functionality. Moreover, when it comes to speeds, you can go up to almost 1.9gbps, when both the bands are utilized, simultaneously. Moreover, the Gig Ethernet support allows you to connect wired devices for eliminating bandwidth compromises.

  • Makes use of Crossband technology for leveraging higher speeds
  • Supports the access point mode via the Gig Ethernet cable

Cons

  • Minor performance issues with other Non-Linksys routers

TP-Link RE590T

If you have a longing for antennas, the new TP-Link RE590T is the best Wi-Fi extender for gaming to purchase. Besides being extremely affordable, this extender is capable of boosting the Wireless bandwidth by a significant margin while covering an area of almost 10,000sqft. Not just that, the existing 700mw amplifiers are capable of achieving boosting speeds of up to 1900mbps.

With minimal bandwidth loss even when connected to the router as a mesh, this innovative gadget from TP-Link is one of the best devices to invest in. While the dual-band support is in sync with the AC1900 standards, the three antennas further validate the amplifying capabilities of the same. You also get access to the Smart Signal notifying mechanism that allows you to select the perfect spot for placing the extender. 

What stands out is the inclusion of a powerful dual-core chipset with a clock speed of 1GHz, capable of processing transmitting logic with ease and precision. However, it is the touchscreen support that is the actual scene-stealer.

Conclusion

While you can select any of the three Wi-Fi extenders for gaming, the final choice depends on your range and budget-specific requirements. For basic home usage, an online gamer can always consider the powerful Netgear EX7300.

The Linksys RE7000 is all about the easier setup, plug-and-play functionality, and exceptional coverage. Last but not least, if you are looking for the best of both worlds, i.e. range, and budget, the TP-Link RE590T is the best product in the market.
