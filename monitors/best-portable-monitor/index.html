Portable Gaming Monitor

Press down on your brakes if you’ve been looking all around for a portable monitor that can fully cater to your gaming adventures. The Hori portable gaming monitor offers clear and ambient pictures that allow you to play those wonderful games even when you’re on the move.

This portable gaming monitor is 15.6 inches and it features an ultra low-lag and an impressive refresh rate. It has a slim and portable design with a microfiber liner, a leatherette built-in protector, and adjustable angle ridges. Its lightweight and compact size make it easy to be folded into premium cases and placed securely in a bag for portability.

This portable monitor has other inbuilt features that make it ideal for gaming and one of such is its built-in speakers and headphone outputs. It functions through its dual HDMI ports via a short HDMI cable that comes with it and it can easily connect between two HDMI compatible devices and AC adapters.

Azuai Portable Monitor 

Portable Monitor For Office Use

Additional Images
Buy from Amazon.com
 

One of the endearing benefits of a portable monitor is the flexibility and comfort that it offers to its users when compared to working with a laptop or a PC, and the Auzai portable monitor employs the best features to address that. This 15.6 inches monitor delivers clear and beautiful visual scenes at a response rate of 60Hz. It employs an IPS panel and HDR technology to produce brighter and more realistic pictures without color shift and it also comes with an on-screen display menu that can be used to adjust the volume and brightness.

This portable monitor has a 178° ultra-wide viewing angle and a magnetic inclusive case that helps the monitor stay sturdy when in use. Its lightweight and the screen protector makes it the ideal portable monitor for taking with you on business trips, school, or simple travels.

This monitor can also be utilized for other relaxing purposes such as gaming and it has a built-in stereo speaker that makes it adaptive for such. The Azuai portable monitor is compatible with devices such as laptops, phones, Xbox, and PS4 via a full-featured USB type C, Thunderbolt 3, and HDMI ports.

Gechic Portable Monitor 

Portable Monitor For Photography & Videographery

See larger image
Additional Images
Buy from Amazon.com
 

You just can never miss that perfect shot with the Gechic 1101P portable monitor. It has a compact and sleek design that makes it ideal for photographers and videographers. It has an IPS panel that displays 16.8 million beautiful colors and these colors are fully optimized to produce a very clear and stunning image.

This 11.6 inches monitor has multiple display connections such as a micro HDMI, a visual graphic adapter, a USB power source, speakers, and a headphone output. It supports 50Hz and 60Hz video input from PCs and 24-30Hz inputs from cameras.

It comes with a tripod mount kit that offers the ease and flexibility of attaching the monitor onto a tripod or an SLR camera. The tripod mount kit has ¼ inch screw holes that fits tightly to the monitor when attached to it. This portable monitor is functionally compatible with laptops, mini-PCs, MacBook, iMacs, smartphones, Xbox One, and Nintendo Switch.
