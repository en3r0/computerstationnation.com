Stunning visuals, great durability, the best viewing angles, and zero tolerance for motion blur are some of the remarkable reasons why the rush for OLED monitors are on the rise.

OLED monitors offer an astonishing display without motion blur or lag and with an incredibly fast response rate to give you an impeccable gaming session. With a high contrast ratio, and brightness for a well-improved image quality that is astonishing, this monitor allows ultrathin, foldable, flexible, and transparent displays on its screen and this feature is something you won’t find easily anywhere else. However, if you are looking somewhere else, try the best overall monitors page.

Another additional benefit of using an OLED monitor is its astonishing brightness that requires no backlighting at all. This feature makes it consume less energy and that means your monitor will be serving you for longer. OLED Monitor for General Computing

Additional Images
Buy from Amazon.com
 

This monitor employs OLED display technology together with its 4k resolution to provide clear and realistic pictures that are immersive from any front angle view. It uses an IPS (In-plane switching) panel to deliver premium picture quality with stunning colors and a 178° viewing angle.

This Philips monitor has an ultra-narrow border that ensures minimal visual distraction, and increases the viewing size of the monitor, making it a preferred choice for dual monitor setup. This monitor also features a multi-view display that enables dual view from different sources at the same time, in a comfortable way.

The picture quality of this monitor employs a 10- bit display that delivers rich color depths with 1.074 billion colors and 12-bit internal processing for smoothly recreating natural colors.

The features utilized by this keyboard to provide well detailed and crystal clear images makes it ideal for 3D graphics application, spreadsheet solutions, web browsing, photography, movies, games, and of course general computing. It functions via two HDMI ports and a display port. The Philips 276E8VJSB is backed up by a 4-year replacement warranty.

The HP pavilion is a great monitor that utilizes OLED display technology in displaying stunning and immersive visuals both in games, office work, and general computing.

It has a refresh rate of 65Hz and a response time of 2ms response time on the overdrive and a 5ms (GtG) response time. It also functions on a plug and play mode that is compatible with all operating systems. This monitor features a 2k (1440P) resolution in delivering well-detailed pictures that are clear and life-like, and also providing a large amount of screen real estate for efficient multitasking. It employs an IPS panel technology for outstanding picture quality with a 178° viewing angle.

Its effective utilization of OLED display technology makes it’s visuals sharp, with rich contrast and brightness which can be easily seen even in well-lit rooms. The HP 27Q is energy star certified and uses less energy which makes it very durable and highly efficient. It also uses anti-glare technology with 3.7 million pixels. It can be connected via it’s HDMI and Display Ports.

Asus VE248H

OLED Gaming Monitor

See larger image
Additional Images
Buy from Amazon.com
 

The Asus VE248H OLED monitor offers stunning images on every frame. It has a slim design and uses a 1080p resolution that offers clarity and premium picture quality on each frame. It has a refresh rate of 240Hz and a response speed of 2ms (GtG) that eliminates ghosting, and blur for a smoother gaming and viewing experience.

This monitor also employs a video intelligence technology that optimizes image quality with intelligent color, brightness, contrast, and sharpness for stunning output. This feature is complemented by Asus Smart Contrast Ratio that enhances and adjusts picture contrast to a preferred mode for more realistic picture quality.

This 24-inch monitor also boasts of six preset video modes and three skin tone modes that can be easily selected via the hotkey on the onboard monitor menu for canes and movies without any loss of data or images. It also utilizes an AI light technology that automatically detects surrounding lights and adjusts the screen brightness so that your eyes can comfortably adapt to the display’s lighting.

The Asus VE248H is VESA wall mountable and its functional ports include an HDMI port, a DVI-D, VGA, and two built-in speakers to enhance gaming and video sounds. It is well designed with a sturdy stand that hides all the cables and it is covered by a 3-year warranty.

Pros

  • It has six preset video modes
  • It employs a Video Intelligence technology that ensures premium picture quality

  • It has a thick frame
