I don’t want to refer to ultrawide monitors as a blessing in disguise because their sizes give them away, there is no disguise. They are big and very noticeable, announcing their presence in a room while offering such immersive and seamless viewing experience that you can’t help but fall in love with them.

Ultrawide monitors usually have a ratio of 21:9 and the large screen real estate makes it ideal for multitasking as well as an immersive view for games and videos. Unlike a dual monitor setup that can be tricky to set up and you’ll still find bezels in the spaces between each screen, an ultra-wide monitor is a single solution to those problems. If you are looking for the best overall monitors though, check out that page.

The resolution employed by ultrawide monitors offers an efficient increase in productivity and a competitive advantage in gaming. Ultrawide monitors graciously earn all the hype associated with them. If you’re hoping to invest in an ultrawide monitor for your games, movies, or work, you should check out our top 3 picks for the best ultrawide monitor. With this buddy standing on your desk, you never miss any action on your screen. This Samsung 43″ ultrawide curved monitor has a bezel-less design and a ratio that is equal to two 24 inch monitors. Its flicker-free visuals are displayed in a curved design that your eyes can easily adjust to, plus it has an eye saver mode that reduces blue light.

This monitor employs a 120Hz refresh rate to produce images that are stunning, and clear. The refresh rate can be adjusted to 60hz and the screen of this ultrawide monitor can be split and two different inputs from two PCs which can be viewed simultaneously. It also employs an easy setting box software that splits the monitor’s workspace into six windows when activated.

This monitor also features a built-in KVM switch that allows the user control and multitasks with two devices that are connected to the monitor via a keyboard and a mouse.

The Samsung CJ89 utilizes two USB type C connections to transfer data, video, audio, and charge. It also has an HDMI port, two USB C ports, and a display port. It has a height-adjustable stand that can also swivel and tilt. The stand can also be mounted on VESA- compatible arms.

LG 38UC99

38 Inch UltraWide Monitor 

See larger image
Additional Images
Buy from Amazon.com
 

This 38-inch curved ultrawide monitor creates an immersive and seamless viewing experience with a fast visual response time of 1ms and its 4k resolution that offers clarity and details in its stunning visuals. The large screen of this monitor makes it a very nice choice if you prefer a singular monitor setup to a multi-display set up. Its display is ideal for designers, gamers, photographers, video editors, producers, and other creative professionals who want a large workspace on their monitor as well as those who desire a premium viewing experience. The screen of this monitor can also be customized for multitasking with the screen split 2.0 that offers fourteen options including four picture in picture sizes.

The LG 38UC99 has 99% coverage on the sRGB color spectrum to produce high-end realistic colors, with the right amount of brightness and contrast for darker colors.

It also employs Free Sync technology for a tear-free and stutter-free visual experience by eliminating choppy frame rates, tear, ghosting and blur in videos and games. This feature employed by this monitor provides a smooth viewing experience even in advanced graphics and fast-paced games.

The LG 38UC99 also boasts of a Bluetooth connection, two built-in 10 watt speakers that produce sounds that are highly appreciated by gamers, producers and video editors. This monitor also utilizes an on-screen control that provides easy access to volume, brightness, presets, and other adjustable features.

It functions via a USB type C port for quick transfer of data, videos, and pictures.
