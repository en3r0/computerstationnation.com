Asus VG279QM

280Hz Refresh Rate Monitor For Gaming

See larger image
Additional Images
Buy from Amazon.com
 

This monitor is built to function at an incredibly fast 280Hz refresh rate for outstanding fluidity in gaming visuals and a 1ms ( GtG) response time that prevents motion blur.

These features ensure superior and lag-free picture quality with outstanding color that is aided by a 72% NTSC color gamut and an excellent 1000: 1 contrast ratio, and HDR400 that delivers a wider color range and higher contrast, an adjustable HDR mode based on the current viewing scenario, shadow boost technology, and a wide 178° viewing angle that ensure minimal distortion and color shift from diverse viewing positions. 

Its display capacity is aided by a fast IPS display that allows the display’s liquid crystal elements to switch up to four times faster than conventional IPS panels for improved response.

This monitor is built to gaming specifications by integrating features such as integrated Game plus hotkeys that offer in-game enhancements to maximize other gaming features fully, and a unique Asus Game Visual that can be easily accessed through a hotkey or the on-screen display settings menu that features seven presets display modes to optimize visuals for different types of contents. 

Its gaming capacity is supported by an Nvidia Gsync compatibility that synchronizes by default with a variable refresh rate ( VRR) technology to sustain a tear-free and lag-free gaming experience from an Nvidia GeForce 10, or Nvidia GeForce RTX 20 series graphics card.

It also functions with extreme low motion blur technology that functions with four different filter settings, flicker-free technology, and adaptive sync that simultaneously eliminates ghosting and screen tear.

The high-end features integrated into this gaming computer monitor make it suitable for first-person shooters games, real-time strategy, and sports titles. It features an extensive option of input ports that includes a DisplayPort 1.2, HDMI 2.0X2, and an audio port that supports a wide array of multimedia devices.

Pros and Con

Pros

  • It is equipped with Gsync.
  • It has a 1080p resolution.
  • It has GamePlus hotkeys

Cons

  • It is not VESA wall mountable.
  • The stand does not support adjustable features.

Alienware AW2521HF

IPS Technology Monitor For Gaming

See larger image
Additional Images
Buy from Amazon.com
 

Alienware prides itself as the official PC and display partner of the league of legends world championship, thanks to its excellent MasterCraft in building high-end gaming accessories that are suitable for different types of games, and this excellent gaming prowess is integrated into the design of the Alienware AW2521HF which makes it suitable for high-end games.

The Alienware AW2521HF computer gaming monitor functions with 99% sRGB color coverage and IPS technology that helps to maintain image clarity from every angle of the screen. Its excellent picture quality is displayed with a 1080p resolution that provides a pixel-per-inch ratio and a bezel-less screen.

Its gaming fluidity is aided by a 240Hz refresh rate and a 1ms response time that eliminates ghosting and motion blur for clear images and smoother gameplay.

The excellent gaming performance of this computer monitor is aided by an AMD FreeSync Premium and Nvidia G-Sync compatible technology that synchronizes the graphics and monitors for tear-free images to prevent lag. It also features other excellent game-centric features such as customizable preset game modes, an FPS counter, easy to use dashboard, and guiding lines for multi-screen setup.

It functions with a unique cooling and venting design at the top, bottom, and rear side to provide an optimized ventilation system that keeps the monitor cool as well as a customizable lighting system with dynamic lighting effects and an ergonomic stand. 

 The input ports built into the Alienware AW2521HFincludes USB 3.0 Gen 1(X2) with power charging,  VESA mount holes, headphone jack, two HDMI ports, a display port, an audio line-out, and VESA mount holes.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • It is freesync premium and Gsync enabled.
  • It has a 1080p resolution.
  • It has a height-adjustable stand 

Cons

  • The stand is not fully adjustable.
  • No integrated speakers.

Pixio PX277

1440P Resolution Monitor For Gaming

See larger image
Additional Images
Buy from Amazon.com
 

The Pixio277 prime computer gaming monitor is built to function at a speed of 165Hz refresh rate and a 1ms response time for gaming fluidity and faster response in action games. 

Its visual quality employs a 111%  sRGB IPS technology, a 1440p resolution, and 350 nits of brightness that increases the dynamic range for incredible clarity and details.

The visuals displayed on this monitor are supported by low blue light and a flicker-free technology that protects the eyes from fatigue and strains caused by exposure to blue light.

The fluidity of the game-centric features is supported by AMD Radeon FreeSync Premium technology and G-Sync compatibility that eliminates choppy frame rates, lag, and screen tear to ensure fluidity every game session. 

The input ports built into the Pixio PX277 includes two HDMI ports, a display port, an audio port, and 100mm X 100mm VESA mount holes. It also comes with DP cables, power cables, and a stand. 

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • It has a 1440p resolution.
  • It is Freesync premium enabled.
  • It has Vision care technologies.

Cons

  • The staff is not fully adjustable.
  • It does not have speakers.

Conclusion

The best computer monitors for gaming integrate high refresh rates for smoother gameplay and reduced motion blur, a synchronization standard for the frame rate of the monitor and graphics card to reduce screen tear and stuttering, a low response time that shows your actions in real-time to aid faster response of in-game actions, high resolutions, accurate color technologies, and vision care technologies.

Gaming monitors are built to cater extensively to pro gamers’ needs by integrating features that are geared towards providing a pleasurable gaming experience, and I hope that this article helped you choose among the plethora of options that are currently available in the market.
