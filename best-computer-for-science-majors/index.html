While you may need the overpowered gadgets used for machine learning and data science, a good computer with an exciting processor and ram will do just good the work at hand.

Dell XPS 15

3D Modelling Supported Computer For Science Majors

The edge-to-edge display of the Dell XPS makes it an awesome computer with many good parts, and these good parts are just what you will find out in this review.

The storage is backed with a 512 SSD, a comforting storage space for everything you will want to save for later and for programs, you will need to install to achieve your student needs as a science major.

The 16GB Ram adds sauce to the already large storage, the machined aluminium body of this computer will give you a perfect feel when you work with the computer.

There is more, with an eye-safe system on this computer, your eyes are given a prolonged safety when you work for those longs hours. The computer supports 3D modelling and other graphic sensitive programs, while the processor is backed with the 10th generation core i7 processor.

This is not all, the computer has a dedicated NVIDIA graphics card for top-quality visuals and display when you work on your computer, on top of this, is the 12.5 hours of battery support.

Lenovo Yoga C930

4K Touchscreen Computer For Science Majors

The 8th generation core i7 processor on this computer does great and makes it a fantastic option on this list, with its terrific processing speed and very accurate display.

The 13.9 inch 4k UHD touchscreen with a fabulous 3840×2160 resolution is what you would not want to miss, the 2-in-1 convertible laptop allows you to take notes and study on the go, without needing to press the keypad all the time.

There is an Intel HD Graphics 620, to take care of all your graphics sensitive applications and needs. The backlight support will allow you to study under dark light conditions and when you are outdoor, the battery affords you over 10 hours of support.

You will not have to worry about your flies or your memory getting filled at any moment, as the Lenovo Yoga is powered by a 1TB storage which gets all your files in one location, while the 16GB of Ram, will guarantee your programs are running at record speed.

Conclusion

For its all-purpose built, and large storage, the giant killer Lenovo Yoga C930, stands tall above its equals on this list as a device to beat for superior quality and efficiency.

You will need the Yoga as it affords a convertible touchscreen display with amazing specifications that beats the others.
