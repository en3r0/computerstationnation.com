Having used a good number of Corsair Mechanical keyboards, my  opinion on Corsair keyboards can be summarized with an extract from a popular novel;

“All keyboards are equal but some keyboards are more equal than the others”.

Corsair does well to sit comfortably among the keyboards that are renowned for gaming and typing by employing incredible features and an ergonomic design that is highly durable and sturdy.

They employ the best keycap designs, RGB lightings, macro keys, sturdy frames, a wrist rest that can be found in over 90% of their keyboards, a customizable software that is well designed and one hundred percent genuine Cherry MX switches that serve you well.  Some of their products might be expensive but there are options that are pocket friendly without compromising on the superior quality that they are known for.

Second to none, Corsair has got the upper hand when it comes to mechanical keyboards, renowned for their responsiveness and unquestionable durability and if you’ve decided to purchase one, you’re making no mistake at all. Like every top brand that has diligently made a name for themselves, finding a preferred Corsair Mechanical Keyboard might be pretty challenging, that’s why I decided to pen down my 3 picks for the best Corsair Mechanical keyboards. The selected products are of very good quality and I’m very sure you’ll love them.

Corsair K65 Lux RGB

Compact Mechanical Keyboard

See larger image
Additional Images
Buy from Amazon.com
 

This compact mechanical keyboard has a tenkeyless design that supports a vibrant gaming atmosphere. It’s large and well textured FPS and MOBA keys feature full key rollover on USB and 100% anti-ghosting features that provide accuracy when gaming. This keyboard also has a USB pass-through that is well-positioned to give you an uninterrupted gaming session. It uses 100% Cherry MX Red Switches with gold contacts for fast, and precise keypresses.

Another interesting feature of this keyboard is it’s vibrant five level multicolor RGB lightings. The LED backlights are very impressive and can be adjusted to three intensity levels and in a situation where you wish to turn it off, you can do so. The Corsair K65 Lux RGB proudly boasts of iCue that allows for customizable advanced macro and lighting programming.

To ensure full control of your gaming arena, the windows key can be deactivated, providing you with uninterrupted thrilling sessions. All the jaw-dropping features are enclosed in an aircraft-grade anodized brushed aluminum frame that attests to its durability.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • The fonts on the key caps are very large
  • It is compact with a tenkeyless design
  • It features per key RGB lighting

Cons

  • The wrist rest is not made of leatherette
  • This keyboard only allows five levels of lighting effects

Corsair K63 

Mechanical Keyboard with Lapboard

See larger image
Additional Images
Buy from Amazon.com
 

Corsair has never ceased to amaze me with their jaw-dropping quality products. This mechanical keyboard is attached to a lapboard, plus it is wireless and this makes it very easy to carry around and use comfortably. The keyboard has a tool-free design that makes the keyboard easily detachable from the lapboard for single use without hindering the functionality of the mouse pad.

It can be used either as a USB wired Keyboard or as a wireless Bluetooth mechanical keyboard. It connects using ultrafast 1ms 2.4GHz gaming optimized wireless technology and has an LED indicator that turns solid blue when paired successfully. This aluminum framed keyboard is light weight and ergonomically built with a built-in wrist rest and a memory foam cushion.

It is compatible with Xbox One and has 100% Cherry MX switches and supports a per key blue LED backlighting. It has a battery that can last up to 15 hours of continuous battery-powered wireless gaming, although this feature depends on the backlighting. 

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • It has a wrist rest
  • It can be used in wireless or wired mode
  • It supports a per key RGB lighting

Cons

  • It requires Windows 8 or 10 for Bluetooth connectivity
  • It has a singule backlighting option

Check it out on Amazon

Corsair K55 Mechanical Gaming Keyboard

 Keyboard and Mouse Combo

See larger image
Additional Images
Buy from Amazon.com
 

Take a bigger and clearer step towards winning with the Corsair k55 gaming keyboard and mouse combo. This keyboard boasts of a 6000 DPI performance gaming mouse sensor and you do not need to modify the mouse sensitivity through the control panel. The mouse also has beautiful customizable RGB backlighting options. It has molded rubber grips by the side that allows you to confidently hold the mouse.

The keyboard and mouse combo is designed with gaming features to help you take the lead when gaming. It has six programmable macro keys that enables key remaps and key combos for executing complex actions. It also has a detachable wrist rest that is designed with soft rubber texture that provides a comfortable position for long and intense gaming hours.

An added feature to this keyboard is the multimedia and volume keys that allows you to easily control and adjust sounds without interrupting your game. The RGB backlighting is also worthy of applause. It is three zoned with over ten preconfigured vivid RGB lighting modes that you can freely choose from.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • The mouse has a DPI sensor that doesn’t need constant modifications
  • The RGB backlighting for the keyboard and mouse are separate
  • The keyboard has a wrist rest

Cons

  • There is no USB port for mouse on the keyboard
  • The keyboard’s software has to be active before RGB color changes can be effected on the mouse

Check it out on Amazon

Conclusion

Put a Corsair Mechanical keyboards to the test and you won’t be disappointed. Employing features that are top notch, their quality is at some of the best. If you didn’t find what you’re looking for here, there are more options available on Amazon that you can check out.
