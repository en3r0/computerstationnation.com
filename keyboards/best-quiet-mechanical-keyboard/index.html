I can remember when my friend got his first mechanical keyboard. The noise always gave me a headache and I always looked forward to when he would be done with it. With time, I have come to know that noises are kin to a mechanical keyboard but no matter how much we pretend to love it, a lot of us wish it could be less annoying.

It is practically impossible to get a completely noiseless mechanical keyboard but there are mechanical keyboards that are more quiet and their keypresses turn out to be nothing like what you’re used to. What this means is that you don’t have to be worried about your princely mechanical keyboard being so loud to the point that it makes the people around you wish you should disappear because of your keyboard’s distracting noise.

If you’re tired of apologizing even when you don’t want to because you’re doing something as simple and classy as using a mechanical keyboard, then stop tapping on that noisemaker and check out the 3 best quiet mechanical keyboards. I’m so excited for you because sounds are about to change!

Razer BlackWidow Lite

Quiet Gaming Mechanical Keyboard

See larger image
Additional Images
Buy from Amazon.com
 

This keyboard employs a Razer orange mechanical switch and this makes it a nice choice for gaming.

It produces a silent but tactile feedback with enviable responsiveness for gaming and toned features for typing. The switch employed by this keyboard is the most silent Razer mechanical switch with an actuation force of 45G, an actuation point of 1.9mm with a travel distance of 4.0mm and supports 80 million keystrokes. With a keyboard like this, you definitely do not need soundproof for your room or cubicle when playing games.

This keyboard features O Ring sound dampeners that reduce key noises to its lowest point, causing less fatigue and headache when gaming without in any way providing less gaming thrill. It has a tenkeyless design that aids speed and accuracy. This keyboard employs white LED lighting and RGB colors on each key with room for customization on effects such as the speed, pattern, and level of brightness.

This silent mechanical keyboard has fully programmable macro support through its unique Razer hyper shift software that allows for all keys and keypress combinations to be remapped to execute complex commands. Its portability is credited to the detachable USB cable that makes it a good mobile companion. Despite being highly durable, this keyboard comes with a 2-year manufacturer’s warranty that covers damages and faults.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • It has a detachable USB cable
  • It has a tenkeyless design
  • It employs O Ring sound dampeners that reduce key sounds

Cons

  • It has no RGB effects
  • The backlight does not work on all the keys

Cherry G80 3000 MX Silent Mechanical Keyboard

Quiet Office Mechanical Keyboard

See larger image
Additional Images
Buy from Amazon.com
 

There has to be a level of consideration when working with people. Not everyone loves the sound of a mechanical keyboard and in a situation like this, be kind and use a silent switch. That way you kill two birds with one stone and everyone is happy.

A silent mechanical keyboard that has proven to be worthy in satisfying the no-noise demands of your colleagues and the mechanical feeling of your humble hardworking self is the Cherry G80 3000. It employs a Cherry MX Silent Black switch that is quiet and doesn’t compromise on the long-lasting switch performance of Cherry switches.

It has a lifespan of over 50 million keystrokes with gold crosspoint technology on each key. This keyboard has 104 keys and uses an integrated 2-component stem that minimizes sounds from each keystroke. Its keycaps are laser etched and are highly durable and responsive for long typing sessions. It employs an N-key rollover that allows for multiple keypresses of up to 14 keys at the same time without anti-ghosting. The keyboard has self-cleaning properties and is highly resistant to dust and dirt. It also has a USB 2.0 interface and an adapter for a PS2 interface. It is compatible with Mac operating systems and PCs by simply connecting them with a USB.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Its USB cord is very long
  • It uses an integrated 2- point component stem on its keys
  • Its keycaps are laser etched

Cons

  • The rubber feet are not very functional
  • It is lightweight

Quiet Portable Mechanical Keyboard

So you went on that trip without taking your keyboard along because of the noisy sounds accustomed to mechanical keys. Well, things are about to change. While you pack your bags, make a little room for the Matias FK303 QBT Mechanical keyboard.

It has a compact minimalistic design that brings your favorite keys closer to you. It is wireless and has Bluetooth connectivity that pairs with laptops, phones, and Mac OS with Bluetooth capabilities. It employs quiet click mechanical key-switches that are quiet as a non-mechanical keyboard. Its key travel distance is 3.5mm with a rechargeable battery of 1600 mAh that can provide a typing power for up to 1600 hours although this is subject to LED usage and typing speed. It also has flexible features that allow you to control the screen brightness and volumes and a USB port for charging purposes.
