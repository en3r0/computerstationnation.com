Frequently Asked Questions

Does the desk come assembled or do I need to assemble it myself?

This depends on the manufacturer. Some of these desks arrive already assembled while others require assembly. The latter only exert just a little effort so you don’t need to worry.

Is the desk covered by a warranty?

Likely yes, but do confirm on the product page before purchase.

Can I change the color on the desk?

Likely yes, but this will vary depending on availability.

Are the drawers lockable?

Yes, some of the drawers can be locked very easily if required.

Can I install casters on the desk?

Yes you can. These white home office computer desks are fully customizable according to your preference, but be aware most of this material is not going to be solid wood.

How long does the desk take to ship?

It can take between a day and a week to arrive at your destination depending on your location.
