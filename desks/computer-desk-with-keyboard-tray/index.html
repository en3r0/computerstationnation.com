The Best Computer Desks with Keyboard Trays in 2020

Last Updated on March 18, 2020

When it comes to buying a computer desk, space is king.

The more space you have, the easier it is to work on your startup, or play World of Warcraft with your Dad.

In other words, a computer desk is not merely a furniture item. It adds a lot to decor and can effect the comfort of your everyday life.

If you are looking for the best computer desk with a keyboard tray you are landed at the right spot. We are going to break down the top 3 computer desks with a keyboard trays to help you use your computer in the best possible way.

Don’t forget though, there are 1 bajillion types of other computer desks to consider as well.

Top 3 Computer Desks with Keyboard Trays

These desks not only help you use your computer with better posture but they will look good in a room. You can use them in a little-space, they are ergonomic, and come in at an affordable price bracket. So, without further ado let’s get started.

Sauder Orchard Hills Computer Desk

Check this out on Amazon.

If you are looking for a best-price and overall best-product, do not pass this Carolina Oak finished computer desk. This is not made of solid wood, but the price and quality blend makes this product one of the best in our list. The product comes in at a low-price and has a under desk keyboard tray.

This desk also shares two other drawers for additional storage. The desk does not come with a computer tower storage option, but the desktop surface itself is enough spacious to comfortably handle many towers, especially if you have a small form factor P.C..

Sauder Orchard Hills’ computer desk is an American made product, environmentally conscious, and manufactured with engineered wood. This overall good valued product has a lot to offer under a lower-end price range representation.

This desk shares almost all positive reviews and possesses longer durability compared to others in the same price bracket. Though the lack of solid-wood construction can be a down-side for some, its durability and 5-year warranty can give you a peace of mind.

Pros:

  • Slid-out driver for keyboard storage
  • Two other additional storage drawers
  • Spacious surface to arrange your things
  • Affordable-price

Cons:

  • Not a solid-wood construction

OneSpace Stanton Computer Desk

Check this desk out on Amazon.

See larger image
Additional Images
Buy from Amazon.com
 

If you are searching for a basic computer desk with a slide-out keyboard tray, your search ends with OneSpace Stanton Computer Desk.

This desk comes equipped with the necessary features to fit your needs. The product is designed with simple metal support; smooth black finish over MDF boards. It has a simple look look which could fit well with a variety of decor.

The desk has specifications that are just right for an average user at 27.5” wide and 19” deep. The desk has enough room for your computer and other accessories to put on the surface of the desk. You can store your keyboard in the slide-out keyboard tray, and start your computing chores without any big tension. 

Below, you get a shelf near the floor to put your computer accessories such as computer tower, audio system, printer, and other office supplies.

It does not integrate any additional drawer other than a slide-out keyboard tray, so users feel the need of having another storage option, some users mentioned this as a negative. 

Still, with such an affordable price, it is a good option to go with and fits the needs of basic computer usage. 

Pros:

  • Under desk keyboard drawer
  • All metal construction with black laminated finish over MDF boards
  • A shelf near to floor to store computer accessories

Cons:

  • No additional storage drawer
  • Visibility of cords and cables

Pinellas Collection Computer Desk

If you need something with lots of additional storage options, this Pinellas collection computer desk is a great option to consider. 

This desk has a salt oak finish and is constructed with manufactured wood. It offers a smooth, seamless and durable work-experience.

The desk has a slide-out keyboard tray, a dedicated cabinet for your computer tower and other additional storage. The hutch also offers additional open storage compartment and hidden cable management.

The computer desk looks fantastic, and has 57.36” height, 62.21” width and 23.5” deepth. The keyboard tray is a good size at 27.472” H x 61.846” W x 11.317” D. 

So, if you are looking for a computer desk with plenty of space to store files, and other accessories this Pinellas designed computer desk is a great option to go with. 

Pros:

  • Quality construction
  • Modern style 
  • Many storage options
  • A separate cabinet for computer tower
  • Hidden cable management system

Cons:

  • Higher price

Conclusion

Buying the best computer desk with a keyboard tray can be tricky, especially if you do not know what features you are looking for. You should first analyze what are your personal preferences and requirement before making a final call. 

We have reviewed some of the best options available on Amazon. However, there are always new options that could be better than these. If you want to make sure we didn’t miss any thing, then continue your search on Amazon.
