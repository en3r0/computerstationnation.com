Computer Desk with Recessed Monitor

Last Updated on February 11, 2019

Do you want a computer monitor that sits down into the desk? Well, are you ready to get weird?

Having a recessed monitor desk is not going to be something you see everyday. Unless it is because you work or go to a school that has them. That is about the only place I can personally recall seeing this type of desk, which makes sense that some people would call it “Classroom Furniture”. Nonetheless it can be great in certain types of situations!

This probably goes without saying, but let’s say it anyway – These desks are not going to be great for full time use. They do not allow you to obtain proper posture. This is according to everything I have read, but I am not a doctor, at least not today. If you are not sold on one of these things, you really should check out other computer desk options.

What are the options?

  • Completely Recessed Monitor Desks
  • Partially Recessed Monitor Desks
  • Recording Studio Desks with Recessed Monitors

Let’s take a look:

Completely Recessed Monitor Desks

Why does this desk have a computer from 1900’s sitting in it? Maybe it was the last time anyone bought one of these. I cannot tell if there is glass over the monitor part, but I would sure hope there is. I also have no idea if you could get a modern flat screen monitor into it*. It would be pretty entertaining to order one and try.

I didn’t intend to write so negatively about this, but there really isn’t a way around it at this point. These things are just plain ugly and outdated. If that is not the case, please contact me and show me a pretty one.

*Note: It looks like this model can handle more modern computers. If you are looking for a computer desk with monitor under glass, then that one is likely your best bet.

Flat Panel Monitor Lift

This is probably one of the coolest things you could do to hide your monitor and completely recess it into the desk. 

This looks like it is straight out of an early 2000’s James Bond film. So if you have a lot of time, an old square monitor you don’t look at all the time, and a Desk you want to cut a hole into, then here is your next project.

I am sure somewhere there is a more modern version of this for wide-screen monitors but I did not run across it.

Partially Recessed Monitor Desks

Again the first thing that comes to mind here is a computer lab at a local college. Well that is what they are marketing as so it makes sense as well.

Outside of that use case though, what are you going to do with this? It is not like you can take the monitor away and use the desk then, there will be a giant hole in it! The one thing this does get points for though is nice cable management.

Recording Studio Desks with Recessed Monitors

This is where things start to get a bit more epic. Music and recording stations tend to have a lot going on. You have all sorts of mixing boards. knobs, audio jacks, and things I simply know nothing about. What I do know is that they look pretty awesome when setup nicely.

Take this one for example. It is hard to imagine a more sleek setup. They even have a cool video of it being used. There are always custom solutions for this as well, but if you have the money and nothing better to spend it on then look no further.

In Conclusion

If you are looking for one of these super weird desks then you probably have found most of your options right here. There is not a huge demand for this type of desk, but if you need one hopefully I have helped you get where you are going.
