Corner and L-Shaped Computer Desk Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • Can save on space
  • Better viewing for multiple monitors
  • Desk items can be in closer reach

Cons:

  • Can take up more room than a straight desk
  • You may feel the urge to buy more monitors to fill the space
