Walker Edison Modern Corner L-Shaped Desk

See larger image
Additional Images
Buy from Amazon.com
 

Here is one product from esteemed furniture manufacture that is sleek and minimalistic. This l-shaped computer desk is extremely durable and flaunts a chassis made of strengthened steel. The finishing of the desk is commendable as Walker Edison uses powder-coated paint to render a classy look to the product. Apart from that, the contemporary design further accentuates the credibility of the manufacturer.

If you are interested in a corner desk for your computing and work-centric requirements, this is the perfect product to consider. What steals the show is the safety glass that is used to craft the majority of the working top.  The tempered glass used here is durable and can withstand occasional blows, drops, and hits with ease. Besides the aesthetic brilliance, you also get a sliding slot for your keyboard and an unattached yet functional computer tower tray.

This product features on our list courtesy of the design elements and flexibility of usage. To start with, you can place the unattached CPU stand almost anywhere, based on preferences. Despite this being a heavy product at 62 pounds, the sleek form factor continues to amaze most buyers. In addition to that, the 51-inch working space is decent enough for the displayed price tag.

Pros

  • Plenty of usable table space for work and leisure
  • Industrial-Grade Toughened Glass for added durability
  • Packs in innovative design elements in unattached CPU stand and sliding keyboard slot

Cons

  • The product is one the heavier side
  • Keyboard tray feels smaller than usual

If you are looking for a gamer-friendly l-shaped glass desk, the one from Best Office rightly fits the bill. While the corner design and the l-shaped layout are aesthetically empowering, the free CPU attachment sweetens the pot further for the customers. Moreover, the working space on offer is adequate enough for a general user.

When it comes to the physicality of the product, the executive l-shaped glass desk flaunts a mechanical design that scores high in terms of reliability and structural innovation. The 6mm hardened glass top further improves the credibility of this desk. Besides these inclusions, you also get a thick steel chassis and adjustable pads which render balance to the product even on uneven surfaces.

This product excels in the manufacturing department. Every part of this l-shaped desk is meticulously crafted. The minimalistic outlook is best complemented by the easy to wipe the surface, sliding keyboard section, and seamless installation procedure.

INVIE L-Shaped Desk

Here is a functional l-shaped glass desk with drawers for storing important documents and increasing the productivity of the workspace. This product from INVIE flaunts a trendsetting corner design with a wedge that saves space and comes across with multi-functional attributes. The 56-inch working surface is made of toughened, tempered glass that is water-resistant and completely scratch-proof. Moreover, the surface doesn’t skid despite featuring glass on a metallic frame.

This product from INVIE is utilitarian and extremely sturdy, precisely from a structural point-of-view. While the metallic frame with glass on top renders durability to the surface, the chassis features strengthened metal; thereby featuring a decent weight-carrying capacity. Moreover, this is an easy to clean product featuring modern design elements.

We added this product to our list as it justifies the existing price tag. The product combines several innovative structural elements including the two drawers that are highly desirable additions. Most importantly, the glass surface is heatproof whereas the entire desk frame brings forth welded steel and polished steel. 

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Tempered glass that is heat and water-resistant
  • Durable chassis flaunting high-end steel for added durability
  • Cleaner and modern product lines

Cons

  • Installation can be tricky without power tools
  • Extremely Heavy product

Editors’ Rating – 4.5 out of 5

Check it out on Amazon.

Conclusion

While the mentioned l-shaped glass computer desks are easily available online, the final selection comes down to your preferences and budget. If you are looking for an executive l-shaped glass desk sans drawers, the one from Walker Edison can be considered.

However, if you prefer additional storage space and adequate working area, the one from INVIE is recommended. Lastly, if you are looking for a perfectly crafted corner desk that’s stable on uneven surfaces, nothing beats the product offered by BestOffice, in terms of structural components.

As always, if you did not see something you liked here, please feel free to head over to Amazon and continue your search there.
