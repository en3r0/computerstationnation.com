Things to Consider When Picking a Computer Chair

There are some important factors to consider when you are picking a computer chair and they include:

Its seat height should be adjustable

This is the most important element to consider when choosing a computer chair. Its height should be adjustable enough to help you look directly at the monitor without straining. The computer chair needs to achieve a height that ranges from 16 to 21 inches from the floor.

You should get ample lumbar support

Your lower back is the lumbar region and it must always be supported to ensure your comfort as you work or engage in a gaming session. The chair should provide firm lumbar support through extra padding and ergonomic design. In addition to that, the lumbar section of the chair needs to be adjustable too!

Choose the upholstery material carefully

Gaming and computer chairs are usually made of two types of materials which include fabric and leather. Fabric is soft, pliable and breathable so it is a great material if you intend to spend numerous hours in your chair. However, most fabrics are prone to stains from spilled drinks or food.

Leather is luxurious, supple and durable but it is not breathable. Due to its characteristics, leather is great for chairs where you will spend only a few hours at a time. Notably, leather is easier to clean than fabric. With that in mind, look for a chair whose upholstery material fits your needs.

The width of the seat and height of its backrest

The width and height of a chair are its main dimensions and they play a very important role. You need a chair that will remain comfortable even after long hours of sitting. For this reason, choose one whose height and width complement your physique. As a rule of thumb, the chair’s depth should enable you to sit with your back straight against the backrest and the back of your knees 2 to 4 inches in front of the seat.

Types of Gaming Computer Chairs

There are 3 main types of gaming chairs:

PC Gaming Chairs

The PC gaming chair borrows its main characteristics from the traditional office chair and improves upon them. This chair contains features such as extra cushioning and a bucket-style seat. It also provides lumbar support, adjustable armrests and a reclining backrest.

Platform Gaming Chairs

This type of gaming computer chair resembles a futuristic recliner. Platform gaming chairs sit very low because they are supported by a pedestal base. They provide side pockets, headphone jack ports and can rock back and forth.

Hybrid Gaming Chairs

These are the most popular types of gaming chairs because they combine the structure of PC gaming chairs and comfort of platform gaming chairs. They are raised high on a swivel base and have a unibody frame. Hybrid gaming chairs also contain advanced multimedia facilities such as surround sound speakers and Bluetooth pairing capability.
